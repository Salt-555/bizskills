# Fishing Charter / Tour Operator Niche Validation

## Market Size
- 5,773 US fishing charter businesses (2026, +4.6% YoY)
- Similar numbers for tour/outdoor guide operators

## Buyer Pain Points
- **FareHarbor charges up to 6% on direct bookings** + 1.9% + $0.30 merchant fee
- **Website builder: $5,000/year** (FareHarbor retains ownership — you lose it if you leave)
- **Checkout friction**: 14-second spinning wheel causing 25% sales drop
- **Platform lock-in**: Captains can't own their digital assets
- **Commission marketplaces** (FishingBooker, GetMyBoat) take 20%+ and captains hate it

## What Captains Actually Want (from forum quotes)
> "If you want to do back-end stuff — booking calendars, marketing campaigns, accounting, billing, expenses, taxes, payment processing — as a subscription service and allow me to be the face and front end of my business, you might have something there."

> "Online booking services truly work for widgets. Charter fishing is very personal... there needs to be a level of connectivity that the others do not have."

## Competitive Landscape
| Tool | Pricing | Gap |
|------|---------|-----|
| FareHarbor | Free + 6% booking fees + $5k/yr website | Own your site, flat pricing |
| Bókun | $49-$499/month + 1-1.5% fees | Too expensive for solo captains |
| FishingBooker | 20%+ commission | Captains bypass to pay direct |
| Checkya | 5% transaction fee | Limited features |

## Downloadable Tool Opportunity
- **Gap:** No one-time purchase downloadable HTML tool exists for this niche
- **What to build:** Booking calendar + digital waivers + payment integration
- **Price point:** $49 one-time or $29/month hosted
- **Target:** Solo captains who don't need enterprise features
